Why PrimeVolt

A specialist partner built for Ontario's grid transformation

Ontario's electricity system is moving from gradual expansion into a much larger, more complex phase. Large firms win the prime contracts — but the work generates a deep support layer where responsiveness and sector know-how matter more than scale.

  • Deep technical credibility — 24+ years across 132 kV–500 kV transmission, grid stations and HVDC systems.
  • Ontario-focused — aligned to provincial demand growth, asset renewal and the transmission project pipeline.
  • Clean-energy aligned — supporting a grid that is already more than 90% emissions-free.
