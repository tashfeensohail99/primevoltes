## Go-live checklist

1. **Register the domain** `primevoltes.ca` (CIRA-accredited registrar; an Ontario company meets the Canadian-presence rule). Register 1ŌĆō2 fallbacks (`primevoltenergy.ca`) and redirect them.
2. **Set up email** `info@primevoltes.ca` (Google Workspace or Microsoft 365). Optional: `rana@`, `careers@`, `projects@`.
3. **Add your details** the site leaves as placeholders:
   - Registered Ontario **office address** (footer, Contact map, Google Business Profile).
   - Founder **headshot** (replace the "RZ" monogram on About/Home).
   - **LinkedIn** company URL (footer/social links).
4. **Activate the forms (2 minutes):** get a free access key from [web3forms.com](https://web3forms.com) (enter `info@primevoltes.ca`, no account needed). Then replace `REPLACE_WITH_WEB3FORMS_ACCESS_KEY` in the `access_key` field on `contact.html`, `careers.html` and `insights.html` with your key. Submissions then arrive at your inbox ŌĆö until you do, the forms show a friendly "ready to go live" message instead of erroring.
5. **Deploy** the `website/` folder to a host ŌĆö Cloudflare Pages / Netlify / Vercel (free, global CDN, free SSL) **or** a Canadian host. The included `netlify.toml` / `_headers` / `.nojekyll` cover the common platforms. Point `primevoltes.ca` DNS to it and enable HTTPS.
6. **Submit** `sitemap.xml` to Google Search Console; set up Google Analytics 4 and a Google Business Profile.

> **Tip:** the forms are generated by `build-tools/`. If you prefer, set your Web3Forms key once in `build-tools/build_pages.py` (the `w3()` helper) and re-run `python build-tools/build_site.py` to apply it across all pages at once.
